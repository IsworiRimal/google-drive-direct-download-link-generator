Google Drive Direct Link Generator

This tool allows you to generate a direct download link to files stored in Google Drive. A direct link will immediately start downloading the file, bypassing the preview in Google Drive.

Notes

  • Make sure your file's visibility in Google Drive is set to "Anyone with the link." If it's set to "Restricted," only people logged in to Google and granted access will be able to open the direct link.
  • This only works for uploaded files, not documents created in Google Docs/Sheets/Slides.
  • If your file is very large, the direct link may first open a page saying that the file is too large for Google to scan for viruses. That page will have a button to download the file.

How to Get Your Sharing URL

Follow these steps to obtain your Google Drive sharing URL:

Step 1: Go to Google Drive and right click the file that you want to share, then click on "Share"

Step 2: In the window that comes up, change the access to "Anyone with the link"

Step 3: Click "Copy link"

Step 4: Paste that link into the text box above and click "Create Direct Link" to create your link. Enjoy!

How Does it Work?

This site takes your sharing URL, which looks like this:

https://drive.google.com/file/d/1LDRlyZaZTF-bxHjBDEiL2obMxYHtf3YT/view?usp=sharing

It extracts the file ID, the part between /d/ and /view, and puts it into a new URL like this:

https://drive.google.com/uc?export=download&id=1LDRlyZaZTF-bxHjBDEiL2obMxYHtf3YT

This new URL format causes Google Drive to serve the file directly as a download, avoiding the preview. You can manually do this conversion, but this site simplifies the process. The conversion happens entirely in your browser, and your sharing URL is never sent or stored anywhere.
